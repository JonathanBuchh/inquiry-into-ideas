Imagine You’re An Alien

If I were an alien, I might find Earth’s reliance on the sun to be strange:

There are other ways to use energy, yet everything on the Earth relies on the sun. All food chains on Earth start with the sun, so all living life is reliant on the big ball of gas in the sky. Humans have not figured out how to transfer energy without losing energy. Energy can be created, yet Humans have not realized how to do it and have created “laws of nature” that state that “energy cannot be created or destroyed.” Statements like that are limiting.
